ABOUT US

TO KNOW ABOUT OUR LUXURY STORY

We are a high-end hotel and restaurant with a sleek glass exterior located in Nairobi, Kenya, about 1 km from the Nairobi National Park.

We have sophisticated rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows and vibrant accents; they feature free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs, plus minibars, tea and coffeemakers, and safes; some include park views. Suites add whirlpool baths, living and dining rooms, and butler service.

Parking is free, as is a breakfast buffet served in a chic restaurant with park views. Other amenities include an indoor kids' area, a state-of-the-art meeting room, an outdoor heated pool, and a gym.
